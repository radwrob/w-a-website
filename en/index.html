About us

We are a transport company with extensive experience, operating on the market since 2003. We specialize in both domestic and international transport.

We offer comprehensive cargo transport and we guarantee that all goods entrusted to us are monitored and protected with security from the moment of loading, during transport, until unloading.

For our clients, we have qualified and professional staff. Enormous competition on the transport market encourages us to meet all clients expectations.

We invite you to make familiar with our offer and make contact!

Offer

We invite companies looking for simple, quick and proven solutions for the transport of goods, as well as some carriers with their own fleet, providing transport services at the highest level.

We guarantee timely and reliable services.

We offer:

  • National transport
  • International transport
  • Forwarding

Transportation is carried out using modern fleet of vehicles, equipped with mobile phones and GPS navigation systems, which allows precise location of each vehicle. We provide transport and its organization throughout Poland, as well as Europe.

Our advantage is extensive experience, individual approach to the customer, punctuality and reliability of the services offered.

Fleet

We have the latest generation of DAF XF vehicles that meet the high European standards of exhaust norms - EURO6 and modern semi-trailers of the KOGEL and WIELTON brands. All cars are equipped with a GPS satellite navigation system, ensuring the safety of services provided by us.
